Welcome to My Wikipedia!

My Wikipidea is a personal encyclopedia where I collect, organize, and share articles on topics that fascinate me—from art and science to history, technology, and hobbies. Every page is written in a simple, easy-to-understand way as I develop my web skills using only HTML. This project is both a learning journey and a creative space where I document new facts, tutorials, and ideas that inspire me.

On My Wikipidea, you’ll find:

  • Step-by-step tutorials to help you learn new skills and understand complex subjects quickly.
  • “Did You Know?” facts to spark curiosity and encourage deeper exploration.
  • Quotes and inspiration from influential people who have shaped fields I admire.

As this encyclopedia grows, my goal is to make learning fun, accessible, and rewarding—not just for myself but for everyone who visits. Whether you want to discover something new, revisit a favorite topic, or see what I’ve been working on lately, I hope you enjoy browsing My Wikipidea.
